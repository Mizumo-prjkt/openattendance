OpenAttendance

A free, open-source tool for managing students' attendance records

OpenAttendance is developed and maintained by a small, dedicated organization with the goal of providing powerful tools for the organization at a scale.

The software is provided "AS IS" and free of charge under a permissive license, allowing you to use, examine, and rebrand it to fit your needs. The only restriction is on redistribution; the official and sole source for the software is its GitHub repository.

Official Repository: https://github.com/Mizumo-prjkt/OpenAttendance
